The Conference

Strengthening Academia-Industry Linkages

The primary objective of the conference is to bring together top researchers, academics, industry experts, and innovators in Computer Engineering, Electronics, and Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning (AI/ML) to share ideas and discuss progress that will facilitate the development of next-generation intelligent systems.

The conference combines theory with practical experience, demonstrating how basic research and applied improvements collaborate to address real-world challenges and encouraging individuals from diverse fields to collaborate on solving complex problems.
